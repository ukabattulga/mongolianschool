Our Curriculum

Mongolian School of Colorado is dedicated to the preservation of the Mongolian language and culture within the younger generations. Students are put into different grade levels depending on their speaking, reading, and writing abilities of the Mongolian language. Students are exposed to Mongolian literature and culture at every lesson.
We also celebrate traditional Mongolian and American holidays throughout the year.

Enrichment

We believe that enrichment is essential to the growth of our students. Thus, students are welcome to participate in our extracurricular enrichment activities: chess club, contortion/yoga, music, math classes, etc.
